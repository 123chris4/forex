Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to open an account?

Opening an account with ForexHub typically takes less than 5 minutes. Simply fill out the registration form, verify your email, and you're ready to start trading with our demo account immediately.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit cards, bank transfers, e-wallets, and cryptocurrencies. All transactions are secure and encrypted for your protection.

Is my money safe with ForexHub?

Yes, we maintain strict security protocols including SSL encryption, two-factor authentication, and segregated client accounts. Your funds are protected by our insurance policy.

Can I trade on weekends?

The forex market is open 24/5, Monday through Friday. Weekend trading is not available as major markets are closed. However, you can prepare orders that execute when the market opens Monday.

What is the minimum deposit?

The minimum deposit depends on your account type. Our Starter accounts have no minimum deposit, while Professional accounts require a $100 minimum deposit.

Do you offer a demo account?

Yes! We offer a free demo account with $100,000 virtual funds. You can practice trading without risking real money and test all our platform features.
